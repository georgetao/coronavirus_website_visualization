About This Website

Novel Coronavirus 2019 (Covid-19)

Following the outbreak of the coronavirus in December 2019, the world has gone into a general scare of containing and preventing the spread of the virus. With the number of reported cases growing worldwide, many countries are implementing travel restrictions and quarantines as a precaution to protect the health and safety of their citizens. In our project, we intend to delve deeper into the spread of the coronavirus both within China and internationally. Additionally, since the SARS virus of 2003 has been identified as the most genetically similar virus to Covid-19, we plan on retrospectively comparing the two diseases.

We have divided this analysis into three main sections: the current state of Covid-19, risk factors, and predictions based similar diseases. Please use the sidebar to explore our visualizations.

About Us

We are aspiring data scientists creating visualizations of the current COVID-19 pandemic. Our goal is to better illustrate the extent of the pandemic and better educate the public using our visualizations. Note that we are not medical professionals and these visualizations should not be considered medical advice.
